PhaseIdentificationMethod phase_identification_method = PhaseIdentificationMethod::None

The method to identify whether liquid or vapor phases is stable.

If both vapor and liquid phases are considered in the system, it is recommended to configure a robust phase identification method such as GibbsEnergyAndEquationOfStateMethod for BOTH phases.
